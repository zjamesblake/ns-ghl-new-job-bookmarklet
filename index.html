GHL Job Creator Bookmarklet

This bookmarklet allows you to create a new job directly from a contact page in GoHighLevel.

Instructions:

1. Create a new bookmark in your browser

2. Name it "GHL Job Creator"

3. Copy the entire code below for the URL/location:

How to use:

1. Navigate to a contact page in GoHighLevel

2. Click the "GHL Job Creator" bookmark in your bookmarks bar

3. The form will appear with pre-filled contact information

4. Complete the form and submit

Note: If the bookmarklet doesn't work, open your browser's developer console (F12) to see diagnostic information.
